Github Pages supporting HTTPS certificates this year prompts me to get this personal blog ready (barely) after owning the domain for over 4 years. This site is statically generated with hugo, using the default anake theme. The theme hasn’t been modified at all because I’m bad at doing webpage layout. I believe a blog is about its content and the styling doesn’t matter. However, I would tweak the style a little bit until the website does not look too low-effort.

What I’m thinking of posting here is personal reviews of cool things I bought or used. I cannot buy things, especially off internet, without doing extensive research. This is a personal habit. I’m not sure if it’s a good one or not, but I hope my reviews will provide at least some insights to who stumbles upon my posts. You could expect reviews of anything from shampoo to furniture.

The reason to write in English instead of Chinese is I always awkwardly relate Chinese writing to the time of doing schoolwork when I was in high school. Writing back then wasn’t fun. The content was almost never anything I wanted to write, and it takes too much effort to actually write with pen and paper. I was force to write for the homework and tests.

It has also came to my attention my Chinese sentenses composition has become alarmingly similar to machine-translated English sentences.

至于为什么要用英文，是因为写中文会有种小时候写周记（作文）的奇怪感觉。而且我中文写出来的句子简直就像从英文机翻过来的。。
